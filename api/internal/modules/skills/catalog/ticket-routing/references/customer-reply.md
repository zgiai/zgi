# Customer Reply

## Use When

Use when the user asks for an initial customer reply,客服回复, acknowledgement, or first-response message for a routed ticket.

## Reply Rules

- Acknowledge the issue.
- Restate the key problem briefly.
- Tell the customer the case will be routed to the appropriate team or is recommended for routing.
- Ask for missing information only when necessary.
- Avoid guaranteeing resolution time, compensation, refund, legal outcome, or responsibility unless a policy is provided.
- Keep tone polite, concise, and service-oriented.

## Chinese Reply Shape

Use this shape when Chinese output is requested:

```text
您好，已收到您反馈的【问题简述】。我们会建议将该问题转交【建议处理团队】跟进处理。
为便于进一步核实，请您补充【缺失信息】。如涉及安全风险或影响扩大，请及时告知，我们会建议优先升级处理。
```

## Internal Notes

If the reply is only a draft, say `客户初步回复建议` rather than claiming the message was already sent.

## Clarification Rules

Ask when the reply must include a committed SLA, refund promise, legal statement, compensation plan, or official policy quote but no policy source is available.
