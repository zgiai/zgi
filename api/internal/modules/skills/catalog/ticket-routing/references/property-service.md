# Property Service Routing

## Use When

Use for property customer-service scenarios such as tenant, owner, merchant, park, building, office, apartment, or community service tickets.

## Common Issue Types

- Repair: water, electricity, elevator, air conditioning, lighting, door lock, network, public facilities.
- Complaint: noise, cleaning, security, service attitude, parking conflict, neighbor dispute, construction disturbance.
- Payment: property fee, parking fee, utility fee, invoice, payment record, refund question.
- Access and security: access card, visitor entry, vehicle entry, monitoring, fire control, safety hazard.
- Leasing or merchant service: shop operation, decoration, handover, rent-related consultation,招商 handoff.

## Department Suggestions

- Facility repair or engineering issue: property engineering or maintenance team.
- Cleaning, security, parking, public-area service: property operations or customer service center.
- Fees, invoices, payment records: finance or property fee team.
- Contract, rent, lease, breach, dispute: leasing, legal, or contract owner depending on mapping rules.
- Safety, fire, elevator trapped person, water leakage affecting safety: emergency property operations plus responsible engineering team.

## Urgency Notes

High urgency if there is personal safety risk, elevator trapped person, fire risk, flooding, power outage, major public-area hazard, or broad service interruption.

## Clarification Rules

Ask for building, unit, location, contact method, incident time, photos/video availability, affected scope, and whether there is safety risk when missing and material.
